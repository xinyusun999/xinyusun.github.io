I am currently a first-year Master's student majoring in Applied Statistics at Fudan University. Prior to this, I received my Bachelor's degree in Statistics from the Department of Mathematics at Nanjing University in 2025.

My current research interests lie at the intersection of foundation models and decision-making, with a specific focus on:
* **Agentic Reinforcement Learning (RL)**
* **Multimodal Search Agents**
* **Large Language Model (LLM) Post-training**
* **Unified Multimodal Models**
